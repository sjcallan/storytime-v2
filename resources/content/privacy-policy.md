- Correspondence with us

- Pages and features accessed

- Access times and dates

If you choose to link third-party accounts (such as social media), we may receive information from those services in accordance with their privacy policies.

## 7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

### 7.1 Access and Portability
You can access and download a copy of your personal data through your account settings.

### 7.2 Correction
You can update or correct your account information at any time.

### 7.3 Deletion
You can request deletion of your account and personal data by contacting us or using the account deletion feature.

### 7.4 Opt-Out
You can opt out of:
- Promotional emails (via unsubscribe link)
- Personalized advertising
- Certain data collection (via privacy settings)

### 7.5 Additional Rights for EEA/UK Residents
If you are in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, you have additional rights under GDPR, including:
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to object to processing
- Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

### 7.6 California Residents
California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including:
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
- Right to say no to the sale of personal information
- Right to equal service and price
